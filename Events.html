OPD

The OPD or an Out Patient Department of a hospital is its backbone. It is the section of the hospital where patients are provided with medical consultations, diagnosis and allied services. It consists of general doctors, pediatricians, orthopedics, and diagnostitians, to name a few. This November, Synapse invites you to run your own OPD. We promise it to be nothing short of an adventure which provides you with all you could want from a cryptic hunt, and much more. Join us in teams of a maximum of 3 members and evolve out of your student shells and into the shoes of a doctor.

Contact Ritul Madhukar if you have any questions.
